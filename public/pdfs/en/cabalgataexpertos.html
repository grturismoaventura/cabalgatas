DETAILED ITINERARY

DAY 1

Reception from 3 PM at the GR Montañés complex. 7:30 PM talk by Guides and packing saddlebags. 9:30 PM dinner. Room delivery at GR cabins.

DAY 2

Breakfast from 7:30. 9:30 AM we depart to Portezuelo Ancho by truck (2800masl) where the horses await us. 3 hours journey to Las Yeseras camp, lunch. In the afternoon optional: rest at Laguna del corazón or horse ride to 3 valleys viewpoint (3 hours horse ride). Dinner and Creole campfire.

DAY 3

After breakfast we will prepare our equipment to depart to "Arroyo Trujillo", we will cross the rough hill (3300masl). Cold lunch, you can shower in the stream. 3 hours horse ride, we rest in the afternoon, the next day is the longest. Dinner and campfire.

DAY 4

We have breakfast and depart to Santa Elena valley, where we will reach the border with Chile (3250masl). 3 hours horse ride. Cold pack lunch. In the afternoon we depart to Laguna de Las Cargas, a paradise in the middle of the Mendoza Andes. 4 hours horse ride.

DAY 5

Breakfast and rest in the morning at Valle de Las Cargas. Lunch. In the afternoon we start the return to valle hermoso, 4 hours journey. Dinner and campfire.

DAY 6

Breakfast and we head to the valle hermoso viewpoint; 3 hours horse ride, where they await us with a delicious lunch and vehicles to transfer us to Los Molles. Restorative shower, afternoon snack and farewell dinner.

DAY 7

Farewell with a rich and abundant breakfast. End of service.

HOW TO GET TO THE ESTABLISHMENT

Own Vehicle

Passengers can arrive in their own vehicles to El Montañés located in Los Molles, Malargüe. When taking the horse ride, the vehicle remains safe on the premises of Club Andino Pehuenche.

Want to travel by Bus or Plane?

You can do it! We suggest traveling one day before to San Rafael Mendoza, sleep and the next day take bus from the terminal to Las Leñas - Los Molles.

Company: Iselin

Travel days: TUESDAY, THURSDAY, SATURDAY

Departure time: 10:00 AM

Arrival time in Los Molles: 12:35 PM

You can hire it at the counter or buy tickets in advance through some internet platform (Ex: Central de Pasajes, Plataforma 10, etc.)

IMPORTANT: From Mendoza City or Malargüe there are no services to the Los Molles area. If you want info on private transfers, consult us!

HOW DO I RETURN?

Finishing the service on day 6, transportation service will be available to go down to Malargüe. The cost will be paid on site.

The price is only frozen by paying as follows:
