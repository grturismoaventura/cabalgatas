DAY 1

The GR Turismo Aventura team awaits you in Los Molles from 4 PM for check-in. EVERYONE MUST BRING COMPLETED FORMS (assumption, declaration and medical form). Check-in will be done on a first-come, first-served basis, so we ask for patience. Once all passengers have checked in after 6:30 PM, the guides who will accompany you on this experience will greet and introduce themselves. They give a detailed talk about the route, "Customs" will be carried out: which is the selection of all the necessary elements for the journey that we will carry in the two saddlebags. The rest of the belongings and luggage will be kept safe in the facilities. (Saturday is generally a very quiet day, where the idea is to get familiar with our riding companions and guides. It's also very important to adapt to the altitude of the place). We will spend our first night at the Hostel facilities where we will share a delicious clay oven roasted chicken. (Our distribution method at the Pehuenche Mountain Refuge is women on one side and men on the other) If you prefer a private place, you can opt for a Cabin a few meters from the Hostel by paying for the stay. (Price to consult)

DAY 2

After a good early breakfast, we depart by van to Portezuelo Ancho where we meet our travel companions: the horses. With the help of guides and horsemen, the most suitable one for each passenger is carefully selected. We begin the horse ride. Lunch is a cold meal. We continue with the horse ride until we reach the camp at Valle Hermoso, where we will spend the first night. Together, we prepare the "beds" with tarps, part of the saddle, sleeping bag and blankets. At the same time, the guide's assistant prepares the fire for cooking. The night will be under the stars, campfire and guitar and the silence of the Andes

DAY 3

After breakfast we will saddle our horses to head west, Trujillo stream, cold lunch, rest and we ride to reach Las Piedras Negras, summer pasture post. There we will spend our third camping night (humid area with grass used for grazing animals in summer). Riding times are maintained around 3 hours in the morning and 2 hours in the afternoon.

DAY 4

Breakfast. On that day the mules and saddlebags will not be prepared since we go up to the border with Chile (Santa Elena pass, 3080 meters). Lunch and return to the camp, where delicious fried cakes await us for a snack. Fourth night under our infinite skies. Dinner. In total we will ride about 6/7 hours.

DAY 5

Breakfast, we will depart for the Yesera del Medio post, GR Turismo Aventura Base Camp. Lunch. Cobre river crossing. This last night we will have the famous truco tournament, be careful not to lose, since whoever loses washes the dishes...

DAY 6

Breakfast, last saddling of horses and mules. Already returning we head to our final destination at Portezuelo Ancho, where a delicious lunch, fresh beer and vehicles await us to take us back to the Pehuenche Refuge in Los Molles, which we will be arriving at around 2 PM and 4 PM. Restorative bath. Mid-afternoon. Dinner and farewell.

DAY 7

Farewell with a rich and abundant breakfast. End of Service.
