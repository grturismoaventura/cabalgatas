Meeting Point: El Montañés - Los Molles

DAY 1

We will welcome you at the "El Montañes" restaurant, located in Los Molles, RP 222, Malargüe. From 11 am. Informative and safety talk with the guide. We will have lunch with good creole empanadas and then we will go to meet and get to know the horses to start the tour. We will leave heading to the old slopes of Los Molles, cross the Pedrero stream and pass through the lagoon area. We will return to the facilities where a delicious afternoon snack awaits us. To close the first day we will taste the famous Malargüe goat (consult vegetarian, gluten-free menu) Duration of the horse ride: 2 and a half/3 hours approx.

Horse ride duration: 2 and a half/3 hours approx.

DAY 2

After breakfast at the facilities, we will start the second day of horse riding heading to the "enchanted girl" Lagoon. We will ride through the volcano flow where we will have a beautiful panoramic view of the Los Molles Valley. We will stop for lunch with breaded cutlet sandwiches, brief rest and resume the horse ride to return to the facilities for afternoon snack. End of service. End of service.
